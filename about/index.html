Alright, so what's the deal?

Well, I originally started this somewhat as a joke. I was referencing a joke from Tuesdays with Tay to a friend, and couldn't remember the episode. So I created a tumblr blog listing the details of each episode. After a few episodes, I moved on and completely forgot about it. As I started to make my portfolio site, I realized I didn't have much in terms of Graphic Design to show. So I decided to come back and work to show my skills in Web Design.


On top of that, as an artist something I really admire is the act of archiving niche areas. One of my favorite examples is a video essay about the history of the Sonic the Hedgehog Ice Cream Bar. Because let's be honest, 95% of these questions aren't worth cataloguing.
